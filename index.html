Winsome Young Innovators

December 22-25, 2020 Young scientists of the school bagged several prizes at the Navbharat Nirman Innovative Project Presentation Competition held under the aegis of India International Science Festival 2020. ... [+]Vaishnavi Garg (IX) won first prize in the category ‘Innovating ideas for habitat & water resource management’ and has been awarded a certificate and cash prize of INR 20,000. Apaar Khare (IX) and Anshul Tripathi (VIII) bagged second and third prize respectively in the category ‘Promoting innovative ecosystems for affordable renewable and clean energy solutions’. They have been conferred with a certificate and cash prize of INR 15,000 and INR 10,000 respectively. Mrinalini Vajpayee (VIII) bagged second position the category ‘Strategic climate action plan for sustainable future’ and has won a certificate and cash prize of INR 15,000.

CBSE CENTRAL ZONE CHESS TOURNAMENT

The Under-11 Chess team comprising Parnika Agarwal (Class IV), Anvi Goel (Class VI), Shreya Chakrabarty (Class V) and Mansha Garg (Class V) performed exceptionally well and bagged the bronze medal at the Chess Tournament organised by CBSE from October 4- 7, 2017, held at Rukmini Devi Public School.
